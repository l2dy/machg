Adding a Repository Group

You can add a grouping folder to the list of repositories in the sidebar and arrange your repositories within it.

To add a Repository Group:

  1. Choose Repository > "Add New Repository Group", or right-click in the left sidebar and choose "Add New Repository Group"

  2. Double click on the group title and change it to anything you want.

  3. Drag and drop the group into the location within the left sidebar that you want.
